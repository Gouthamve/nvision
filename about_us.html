ηvision 2015

Techno-Management fest of IIT Hyderabad

Started in 2011, ηvision is the techno-management fest organised by the students of IIT Hyderabad with a motto of providing a platforn to the technical enthusiasts of our country to explore, innovate and showcase their technical and engineering prowess. Over the past 3 years , ηvision has gradually evolved from an inter college festival to one of the recognised techno-management fests of our country.

With a broader scope and bigger ideas, ηvision 2015 looks forward to explore ever widening horizons and surmounting greater heights.

With a myriad of events spread across a plethora of domains, ηvision 2015 promises to be a platform which shall integrate technology with challenging life problems. A festival full of promise, ηvision 2015 shall be a journey more exciting and enthralling than any other to bring out the real geek in any techie.
